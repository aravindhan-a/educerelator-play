Frequently Asked Questions

Is EC Play actually free — no hidden charges?

Yes. All 6,000+ questions across every class and subject are free to play — as many practice sessions as you like, with no ads. A Premium plan with fresh AI-generated questions every day is coming soon; today's question bank stays free.

What age group is EC Play designed for?

EC Play is designed for students from Class 1 (age ~6) through Class 12 (age ~18). The adaptive engine ensures a Class 1 student gets simple, visual questions while a Class 12 student gets appropriately challenging ones. The game mechanics keep younger students engaged without being overwhelming.

Can parents track their child's progress?

Yes. Each student account stores day streak, total questions answered, accuracy percentage and subject-by-subject breakdown. The profile screen shows a 7-day activity calendar. A dedicated parent dashboard is on the roadmap.

Does EC Play work without internet?

EC Play works best with an internet connection. Question banks are cached locally after first load, so a short session is possible after briefly going offline. Full offline PWA mode is coming soon — particularly important for students in areas with limited connectivity.

Is EC Play available as an Android or iPhone app?

Not yet as a native app, but EC Play is a Progressive Web App — open educerelator.com in Chrome or Safari and tap "Add to Home Screen" to install it on your device. It will launch full-screen like a native app.
