How EC Play Works

EC Play uses an adaptive engine — the more you play, the better it understands your level. Get a question right and the next one gets a little harder. Get it wrong and it finds an easier route. This means every student gets a personalised practice session, not a one-size-fits-all quiz.

After each 10-question session you earn a star rating, see your score and track your streak. The home screen shows your running accuracy and day streak so you always know how you are doing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is EC Play free?

Yes — free to play. Over 6,000 practice questions across all subjects and all 12 classes, with no advertisements. A Premium plan with fresh AI-generated questions every day is coming soon; the core question bank stays free.

Which board does EC Play follow — CBSE or State Board?

Questions are aligned with CBSE and NCERT guidelines. State board variants for Maharashtra SSC, Tamil Nadu Samacheer and others are coming soon.

Can I study in Hindi, Telugu or Tamil?

Yes. Every question is available in all 13 languages. Switch language instantly from the dropdown at the top of the page — the question text, answer choices and audio all change to your chosen language.

Do I need to install an app?

No download needed. Open educerelator.com in any browser — mobile or desktop — and start playing immediately. Works on Android, iPhone, tablets and computers.

Is there audio support?

Yes. Each question is read aloud automatically using text-to-speech in your selected language, making EC Play accessible for early learners and students with reading difficulties.

Is EC Play good for competitive exam preparation?

EC Play builds a strong conceptual foundation that helps with competitive exams. The adaptive difficulty and subject-wise question bank are particularly useful for students preparing for Class 10 boards, NTSE, and Class 12 board examinations.
