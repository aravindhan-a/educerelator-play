Educerelator builds EC Play — an adaptive practice platform for Class 1–12 that is free, ad-free, and speaks 13 Indian languages.

Why we exist

Most Indian ed-tech puts learning behind a paywall and behind English. We believe a student in a Tamil-medium or Hindi-medium classroom deserves the same quality of practice as anyone else — without their parents paying a rupee. EC Play's entire question bank (6,000+ CBSE/NCERT-aligned questions across every class and subject) is free, with no advertisements.

What we promise parents

  • Free to play — the core question bank will always be free
  • No advertisements, ever
  • Child-safe: no chat, no strangers, no external links in the app
  • Minimal data: play as a guest with nothing collected, or sign in to sync progress (name, email, progress only — see our Privacy Policy)
  • Every question independently verified for accuracy — and a growing set of checks guards every new batch

What EC Play covers

Class 1 to 12 across Maths, Science, English, Social Studies, Hindi, Tamil and the full Class 11–12 streams — plus revision tracks for NEET, JEE, UPSC, Olympiads and board exams. Everything works in English and 12 Indian languages: Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Odia, Punjabi, Urdu and Nepali.
