Q1. Sociology is the study of: 👥

  1. Society and social relationships
  2. Political systems
  3. Economic theories
  4. Individual psychology

A. Society and social relationships

Q2. The term 'Sociology' was coined by: 👥

  1. Emile Durkheim
  2. Karl Marx
  3. Max Weber
  4. Auguste Comte
Show answer

D. Auguste Comte

Q3. The process by which a person learns the norms and values of their society is called: 👥

  1. Socialization
  2. Stratification
  3. Urbanization
  4. Modernization
Show answer

A. Socialization

Q4. A nuclear family consists of: 👥

  1. Only parents
  2. Parents, children and grandparents

  4. Parents and their children

D. Parents and their children

  3. Meritocratic system

D. Closed system
