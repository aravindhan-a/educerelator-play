Q1. Microeconomics studies the economic behaviour of: 📊

  1. Individual units like households and firms
  2. National income
  3. The entire economy
  4. Government spending
Show answer

A. Individual units like households and firms

Q2. The law of demand states that, other things being equal, as price increases: 📊

  1. Demand decreases
  2. Demand stays constant
  3. Demand increases
  4. Supply decreases
Show answer

A. Demand decreases

Q3. Statistics in economics is primarily used for: 📊

  1. Collecting and interpreting economic data
  2. Predicting weather patterns
  3. Writing government policies
  4. Literary analysis
Show answer

A. Collecting and interpreting economic data

Q4. The central problem of economics arises because of: 📊

  1. Scarcity of resources
  2. Lack of government regulation
  3. Overproduction of goods
  4. Unlimited resources
Show answer

A. Scarcity of resources

Q5. A perfectly competitive market has: 📊

  1. One seller
  2. Few buyers and sellers
  3. Many buyers and sellers with homogeneous products
  4. Government-controlled prices
Show answer

C. Many buyers and sellers with homogeneous products

Q6. Mean of the data set {4, 8, 6, 10, 2} is: 📊

  1. 8
  2. 5
  3. 6
  4. 7
Show answer

C. 6

Q7. Opportunity cost is defined as: 📊

  1. Total expenditure on goods
  2. The value of the next best alternative foregone
  3. The cost of production
  4. The price of a good in the market
Show answer

B. The value of the next best alternative foregone

Q8. Which of the following is NOT a factor of production? 📊

  1. Money

  3. Land

Q9. The median of the data {3, 5, 7, 9, 11} is: 📊

  3. 6

Q10. When supply exceeds demand, the market price tends to: 📊

  1. Remain the same

Q11. The branch of economics that studies individual units like households and firms is:

Q12. A market structure with only one seller is called:

Q13. The desire for a good backed by ability and willingness to pay is called:

Q14. The branch of economics that studies the economy as a whole (GDP, inflation) is:

  1. Microeconomics

Q15. The total market value of all final goods and services produced in a country in a year is called:
