Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

We believe children deserve the same privacy protections as adults — and stronger ones. This policy explains plainly what we collect, why, and how you can control it.

1. Who We Are

Educerelator Play ("we", "our", "the Service") is an adaptive learning platform for students in Classes 1–12, operated by Educerelator (India). You can reach us at hello@educerelator.com.

2. What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to personalise and save your learning progress:

  • Account information: Your name and email address when you register or sign in with Google.
  • Learning progress: Subject scores, streak counts, and session history — stored so progress carries across devices.
  • Language preference: Your selected interface language, stored on your device.
  • Device data: Browser type and approximate region — collected automatically by our hosting providers (GitHub Pages and Firebase) for security and performance. We do not collect precise location.

We do not collect photos, audio recordings, payment details, or any data beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To save and sync your progress across devices.
  • To personalise question difficulty based on your performance (adaptive engine, runs on your device).
  • To send you service emails (password reset, account security). We send no marketing emails.
  • To improve the quality of questions and the product experience in aggregate, anonymised form.

4. Children's Privacy

Educerelator Play is designed for school-age students. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. For students under 13, a parent or guardian must create and manage the account.
  • We do not show advertisements to any users.
  • We do not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties for marketing.
  • Parents may request to review, correct, or delete their child's data by emailing hello@educerelator.com.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following sub-processors to operate the Service:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore database) — stores account and progress data on Google's secure servers. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages — hosts the web application. Subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Anthropic Claude — generates AI-based quiz questions on our backend server. Question prompts are sent server-side; no personal data is shared with Anthropic.

6. Data Retention

Your account and progress data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete all associated personal data within 30 days. You can request deletion at any time by emailing hello@educerelator.com.

7. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Firebase enforces strict security rules so that each user can only access their own data. API keys and credentials are stored server-side only — they are never exposed in the browser.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email hello@educerelator.com. We will respond within 15 business days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. We will post the updated date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify signed-in users by email at least 7 days in advance.

10. Contact

Questions or concerns? Write to us at hello@educerelator.com.
